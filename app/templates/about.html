IPU Results

  • This is an unofficial result portal developed to ease the process of checking the results.
  • Generally, we get results in form of long PDF's which are difficult to read and one can barely calculate the percentage.
  • Predicting the ranks is next to impossible thing! This portal serves all these things.
  • You get a nice marks presentation, percentage, SGPA, and ranks at both college and university level using Portal.
  • You can also generate your profile which gives out all the previous semesters' results and CGPA using Generate Profile
  • You can analyse various trends in this result data using Analytics Dashboard
  • You can view the rank lists with or without various filters using Rank listThe ranks are sorted in decreasing order of Percentage.
  • Reappear Cases are not reflected in this portal nor any marks upgradation. If you want to update your marks here, you can contact me on LinkedIn
  • What's for me in this? Nothing! No ads, no bloatware!
